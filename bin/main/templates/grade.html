Routes of this Grade

There are various grading systems in place for climbing routes.

  • In Sport and Trad, YDS the Yosemite Decimal System: Ranges from 5.0 to 5.15d
  • In Bouldering, the " V" scale or the Hueco "V" grades: Range from VB or V0 to V16
